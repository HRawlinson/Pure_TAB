Conserving energy can be quite simple. Implementing the following strategies could save up to 50% of your HVAC energy consumption.

  • Normal Routine Maintenance can save 10-15% on HVAC bills
    • Checking Belts, Filters, and Coils
  • Additional Care can save 10-20%
    • Better Quality Filters, Ultra-Violet Lighting,
      and Programmable Thermostats
  • Verifying Performance of Equipment can save 15-30%
    • Checking for HVAC Duct Leakage
      and Building Envelope Leakage
    • Checking Equipment Performance
      in Both Heating and Cooling Modes
  • When Replacing Existing Equipment Compare Total Cost Options
    • Initial Cost VS Life-Span Cost
    • Heat Pump VS Split System
    • Gas Furnace VS Electric Reheat
