About Us

PURE Test & Balance LLC is a family owned company based in South Mississippi. We have over 25 years in the Test and Balance industry and energy conservation. Our business is dedicated to teaming up with Building Owners, Real Estate Managers, Architects and Engineers to drive down energy consumption in order to save on operating costs. We perform the testing, adjusting, and balancing of the HVAC system, both air and water. We ensure the system is functioning at peak performance, according to control strategies and to engineer and product specifications. If system performance is below tolerance levels, we discover and disclose the reason why.

Advantages for our clients are:

  • Energy Dollars can become Discretionary Dollars as monthly operating costs are minimized
  • Building Health is improved which consequently reduces employee sick leave
  • Thermal Comfort is optimized increasing employee productivity and morale
  • Complaints and maintenance calls diminish

Why to Choose Us

PURE Test & Balance wants to become a "teamplayer," working together with our client, to reduce their energy bills. We believe that an investigation into the actual field conditions sheds light on system needs. Our perspective is that valid data leads to informed decisions and planned adjustments. This concept obtains more efficient performance from the equipment, thereby reducing energy consumption and improving building health. Performance is the driving mechanism for any HVAC System!
